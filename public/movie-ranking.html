The Best Movie According to Abin

My Top 3 Movie of all Time

Taxi Driver

Travis, an ex-marine and Vietnam veteran, works as a taxi driver in New York City. One day, he decides to save an underage prostitute from her pimp in an effort to clean the city of its corruption.

Release date: 8 February 1976 (USA)
Director: Martin Scorsese
Box office: 2.86 crores USD
Screenplay: Paul Schrader
Distributed by: Columbia Pictures
Cinematography: Michael Chapman

American Psycho

Patrick Bateman, a wealthy investment banker, hides his psychopathic ego from his friends. Later, his illogical fantasies escalate and he submits to an uncontrollable bloodlust.

Release date: 14 April 2000 (USA)
Director: Mary Harron
Story by: Bret Easton Ellis
Adapted from: American Psycho
Distributed by: Lionsgate, Lionsgate Films
Box office: $34.3 million

The Good, the Bad and the Ugly

During the Civil War, two men, Blondie and Tuco, form an uncomfortable alliance while looking for treasure. They must also outwit Angel Eyes, an outlaw who wants to plunder the riches for himself.

Release date: 23 December 1966 (Italy)
Director: Sergio Leone
Music director: Ennio Morricone
Featured song: The Good, the Bad and the Ugly
Screenplay: Sergio Leone, Mickey Knox, Luciano Vincenzoni, Sergio Donati, Agenore Incrocci, Furio Scarpelli
Distributed by: United Artists, Produzioni Europee Associati
